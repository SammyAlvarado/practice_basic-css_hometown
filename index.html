Orange County, California

Orange County is one of those hidden gems of California not known about when first visiting the Golden State. Founded on March 11, 1889, making it one of the oldest counties in Califonia. Soon after its establishment the first city of Anaheim was founded accompanied by the cities of Santa Ana, Tustin, Orange, Westminster, and Garden Grove.
